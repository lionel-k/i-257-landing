Who we are

Dreamers. Doers. Passionate.

We are a network of professionals and students in the domains of Sciences, Engineering, Business, Humanities and Social Sciences, aspiring to bring innovation in Burundi through collaboration, mentorship, and initiatives.

We believe in empowering Burundian youth, so that they shall take responsibility for the development of their country.

We want to be at the forefront of Innovation in our field and use our skills to develop Burundi.

What we do

Share Ideas

We have built a brainstorming map where we share the projects close to our hearts. We commit to make them happen.

Connect People

We are growing a network of high-achieving, motivated and innovative Burundian students and professionals living all over the world.

Gather Knowledge

We have built an education platform, mentorship for personal development, career prep, and a carrefour for opportunities.

Prix Innovation

We have launched a series of Innovate Prizes in different institutions in Burundi to promote critical thinking on social problems the youth face.

- Université du Burundi

- Ecole Internationale de Bujumbura

Look for new projects

We are constantly looking for new collaborations where our members can give a hand to advance the potential in empowering the Burundian youth in all fields.
